About PETBot

Pakistan's Trusted Partner in Plastic Packaging Solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2010 in Lahore, Pakistan, PETBot has grown from a small workshop to a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility spanning 50,000 sq. ft. We specialize in the production of PET, PP, and PC bottles, jars, and complete bottle-making plants.

Our journey began with a vision to provide international-quality packaging solutions to local industries. Today, we export to over 15 countries including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kenya, Bangladesh, and Afghanistan.

With ISO 9001:2015 certification and investments in German and Chinese machinery, we ensure every product meets the highest standards of quality and consistency.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective packaging solutions that empower businesses across Pakistan and beyond, while maintaining the highest ethical and environmental standards.

Our Vision

To be the leading plastic packaging manufacturer in South Asia, recognized for quality, innovation, and customer-centric approach, while contributing to a circular economy through recyclable products.
